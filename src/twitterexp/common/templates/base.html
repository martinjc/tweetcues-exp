About×

The TweetCues Experiment is being carried out by the School of Computer Science and Informatics as part of the "Recognition" project. Recognition is an European Union research project funded under the FP7 Future Emerging Technologies scheme. For more information on the project, please see the project website at http://www.recognition-project.eu.
Created by @martinjc and @waltercolombo for @RecognitionEU

Privacy×

We absolutely will not use, sell or distribute any of your personal data without your express permission.
The only personal data we collect is your twitter username, your twitter id number and a link to your twitter profile image. This data is associated with your responses to the questions in the experiment. This data will not be used for any purposes other than to associate your responses to an individual twitter account.
At no point will we make the fact that you have taken part in this experiment public, all responses will be treated anonymously when reporting results.
We will not contact you in any way following the experiment
If you would like your data to be removed from our system, please email m.j.chorley@cs.cardiff.ac.uk, telling us your twitter username and asking to be removed.

Contact×

To contact us tweet @recogntionEU or @martinjc, or email m.j.chorley@cs.cardiff.ac.uk
To remove your data from the experiment, email the address above with the subject "REMOVE -your_twitter_username-".
